Privacy Policy

이름궁합  ·  Last updated: June 2026

이름궁합 ("we", "our", or "the app") is a name-compatibility fortune game developed by an individual developer. This privacy policy explains what data the app stores and how your privacy is protected.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no account system, no servers of our own, and does not send any data off your device.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Names you enter and your compatibility reading history
  • App settings and preferences (such as auto-save on/off)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses no third-party services. There are no advertising SDKs, no analytics or tracking tools, no in-app purchases, and no communication with any external server.

3. Data Sharing

We do not transmit or share any of your data. The app works fully offline.

If you choose to share a result using the system share sheet, that content is sent only to the app or service you select at that moment. This is initiated entirely by you; we are not involved in the transfer and do not collect or retain anything shared this way.

4. Your Rights

  • Delete your data: You can remove individual readings from the in-app history, or delete all locally stored data at once by deleting the app.
  • No tracking to opt out of: Because the app does not track you or collect any data, there is nothing to opt out of.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal information is collected at all, the app is safe for users of any age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
