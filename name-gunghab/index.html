About

이름궁합 is a digital take on a Korean fortune-telling pastime: enter two names, count the strokes of each Hangul character, and see how well they match. It's a light, playful game meant purely for fun and entertainment.

Pick from several traditional counting rules, try alternative game modes, and save a shareable result card. Everything runs on your device — no account, no internet, nothing to sign up for.

Features

  • Compatibility score from Hangul name stroke counts
  • Multiple counting-rule presets (standard, traditional, and more)
  • Extra play modes including the classic ABC love game
  • Save and share your result as an image card
  • Local history of your past readings
  • Fully offline · no ads · no data collection
